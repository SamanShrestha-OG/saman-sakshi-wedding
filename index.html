Our Story

हाम्रो कथा · हमारी कहानी

His Side — Saman

September 2021. Our best friends were in the middle of a dramatic breakup — the kind with season-finale energy — while I was just trying to mind my business.

Then I saw her. My brain shut down and rebooted with one thought: “Damn!! Ckwaaak.” Don’t ask me why — my heart spoke first.

I came to listen to my friend vent. Instead, I saw Sakshi and instantly forgot who I was. My posture straightened, my voice got deeper, and suddenly I was saying things like “Yeah, I’ve been going to the gym.” (I definitely was not.)

Her laugh hooked me instantly. She was funny, chaotic, calm, dramatic, and gorgeous all at once — a walking plot twist — and I was done for.

And now, after all the twists, airport goodbyes, random city moves, dramatic friend breakups, and one unforgettable “Ckwaaak” moment… here we are, about to start forever.

If our story were a movie, this is where music swells and everyone whispers, “Ugh… they’re so cute.” And they’d be right — because somehow, against all odds and all zip codes, we found our happily-ever-after anyway.

Her Side — Sakshi

What was supposed to be a simple 2021 getaway turned into destiny doing its thing — messy but efficient.

At that time, I was living everywhere: New Jersey → New York → Austin → Pocatello. Basically a full-time nomad collecting zip codes.

Then came Dallas. Not for the weather, or the traffic, or the heat trying to roast me alive… but for Saman.

He made the chaos softer, the moments sweeter, the days brighter. Home stopped being a place. It became a person.

I wasn’t looking for love — but then he said “Hey,” and everything shifted.

And after all the moving and all the miles, I finally stopped running… because I found where I’m meant to stay.

A journey from Kathmandu & Rajasthan to one celebration in Dallas.

A story that started with chaos, laughter, and one unforgettable “Ckwaaak.”

Two hearts, many cities, and one beautiful destination.

We can’t wait to celebrate this love story with you.
