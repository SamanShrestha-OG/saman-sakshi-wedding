Our Story

How we got here

His side

September 2021. While our best friends were very dramatically breaking up, I saw her and my brain went: “...damn. Ckwaak.”

What was supposed to be a normal hang turned into late-night drives, too much chai, and somehow, me accidentally soft-launching my future wife to everyone before she even had a place to live.

Jersey, New York, Austin, Pocatello—she kept changing cities, but somehow always stayed on my screen. In the end, she picked Dallas. I like to think she actually picked me.

Her side

What was supposed to be a quick escape trip turned into meeting the most unexpectedly right person, at the most chaotic time.

Between job changes, cities, and bad Wi-Fi, there was one very steady constant—this calm, ridiculous, kind man who somehow made everywhere feel a little more like home.

From “this is fun” to “okay, this is real” to “fine, Dallas it is,” here we are, inviting you to celebrate the easiest yes of my life.
